Paula Vega

I'm Paula Vega, a fashion stylist based in Spain. With a background as a styling assistant and lead stylist, I blend strong visual storytelling with a deep sensitivity to detail, creating editorial, commercial, and audiovisual looks that feel both current and uniquely expressive. I've collaborated with talents like Judeline, Sila Lua, and Bely Basarte for platforms such as Vogue España, Folie Magazine, and Somos Nuebo, always aiming to shape each project with authenticity and a clear creative vision.
